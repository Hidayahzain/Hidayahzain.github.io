What Can I Do

Our breads are crafted with the highest quality ingredients, and range from traditional French baguettes to naturally leavened ancient grains. Our bakers strive to perfect traditional recipes, while adapting them for a modern consumer.

Quality & Freshness

Select premium natural ingredients such as flour and pure butter from New Zealand on a global basis. Ingredients are specially prepared from its raw state to produce mozzarella or fillings in our Kets Kitchen, ensuring a higher quality control of the finished products.

Well Made Recipes

Focusing on creating perfect harmony between textures, flavors and ingredients, the recipes are inspired by Baker's personal approach to classic simplicity.

Joyful Experience

Not only do we want to bring great food, we want to smile at the faces of our customers. Whether it's breakfast, lunch or dessert, Knead Bread wants to be the flavor that brings joy to your day.
